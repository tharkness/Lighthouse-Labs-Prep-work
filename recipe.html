Spamghetti

Spamghetti is a simple and easy to make meal that can stay in the fridge for a couple days. If you are looking for healthy food, look somewhere else.

Ingredients

  • 2 cans of Spam
  • 1 bag of spaghetti
  • 1 can of tomato sauce
  • 1 teaspoon of margarine
  • ketchup

Preparing to Cook

  • Fill a mid sized pot to about halfway
  • Turn heat to maximum until water boils

Cooking the Pasta

  • When the pasta boils throw the bag of spaghetti in
  • Bring the heat down to low and cover
  • Slice up the spam as desired
  • Turn on the TV and wait until the pasta has reached desired softness

Preparing the Sauce

  • Drain the water out of the pot
  • Dump the can of sauce in
  • Add a teaspoon of margarine
  • Add ketchup as desired
  • Set heat to low
  • Stir pasta until sauce is spread evenly
  • Add spam and continue stirring
  • When you feel the heat rising from the pasta, it is ready to serve
